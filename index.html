About us

Elevate Academy of Technology & Innovation (Elevate) (formerly Rambutan Code Academy) is an upskilling and reskilling school, focusing on emerging technologies and innovation.

Founded in early 2019, Elevate is a Sabahan company and based in Kota Kinabalu, making Elevate the first coding school in Borneo (Sabah and Sarawak).
